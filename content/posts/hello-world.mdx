# Hello, World.

This is the first post on this site. I'll use this space for short-form
writing on the things I spend most of my time on:

- **Security research** — walkthroughs of things I've broken (or tried to).
- **AI × Security** — notes from the intersection where both sides are moving fast.
- **Systems & tooling** — whatever infrastructure rabbit hole I'm in.

The goal is to write more, think more clearly, and (hopefully) help a few people
along the way. If you're reading this, thanks — and if any of it connects with
your work, I'd love to hear from you.
